Awesome List of Text Books for Structural Engineers

Vijay Kumar Polimeru
3 min read February 1, 2020

Categories

  • PG
  • Research
  • Text Books
  • UG

Tags

  • Research
  • Text Books

Following are some of the awesome text books for learning various concepts of structural engineering (from very basic to advanced). (Note - Text books are arranged in random order, it does not signify any hierarchy). If any text book is missing from the list, please suggest the name of the book and author (any web-site link if possible) in the comments section…

Design of Multi-storey buildings

  1. Illustrated Design of Reinforced Concrete Buildings (Design of G+3 Storeyed Buildings + Earthquake Analysis & Design) - Dr. S. R. Karve and Dr. V. L. Shah
    1. Do not afraid - Preferable for UG, PG and Practicing engineers
  2. Structural Design of Multi Storeyed Building - U. H. Varyani
    1. Do not afraid - Preferable for UG, PG and Practicing engineers

Engineering Mechanics - Statics and Dynamics

  1. Engineering Mechanics - Statics and Dynamics, - RC Hibbeler
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  2. Vector Mechanics for Engineers: Statics and Dynamics - Beer and Johnston
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG

Mechanics of Materials and Strength of Materials

  1. Mechanics of Materials (SI Units) - R C Hibbeler
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  2. Mechanics of Materials (SI Units) - Beer and Johnston
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  3. Mechanics of Materials 8th Edition - Gere and Goodno
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  4. Strength of Materials - (Part - 1) - Stephen Timoshenko
    1. Be careful - Preferable for only PG

Theory of elasticity and Plasticity

  1. Strength of Materials : Advanced Theory and Problems (Part - 2) - Stephen Timoshenko
    1. Be careful - Preferable for only PG
  2. Computational Elasticity - Mohammed Ameen
    1. Do not afraid - Preferable for only PG
  3. Advanced Mechanics of Materials - A. P. Boresi
    1. Do not afraid - Preferable for only PG

Structural Analysis

  1. Structural Analysis - Prof. Devdas Menon
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  2. Advanced Structural Analysis - Prof. Devdas Menon
    1. Do not afraid - Preferable for only PG
  3. Structural Analysis - R C Hibbeler
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  4. Structural Analysis - Vol. 1 - S. S. Bhavikatti
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  5. Structural Analysis - Vol. 2 - S. S. Bhavikatti
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG

Pre-stressed Concrete

  1. Prestessed Concrete - Prof. N. Krishna Raju
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  2. Prestressed Concrete Design, - Prof. Praveen Nagarajan
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  3. Prestressed Concrete Structures - Prof. Collins and Prof. Mitchell
    1. Be careful - Preferable for only PG

Reinforced Concrete Design

  1. Reinforced Concrete Design - Prof. Menon and S. Pillai
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  2. Reinforced Concrete Design - Prof. N. Krishna Raju
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  3. Unified Theory of concrete Structures - Prof. T. T. C. Hsu and Prof. Y. L. mo
    1. Be careful - Preferable for only PG

Steel Design

  1. Design of steel structures (Limit State) - S. S. Bhavikatti
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  2. Design of steel structures (Limit State) - Prof. Subramaniam
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  3. Design of steel structures - Prof. S. Duggal
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG

Finite Element Analysis

  1. Finite Element Method with Applications in Engineering - Y M Desai, T. L. Eldho and A. H. Shah
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  2. Finite Element Method in Engineering - S. S. Rao
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG
  3. Finite Element Analysis - Prof. P. Seshu
    1. Do not afraid - Fundamentals are clearly explained
    2. Preferable for both UG and PG

Structural Dynamics

  1. Dynamics of Structures - Prof. A. K. Chopra
    1. Do not afraid - Preferable for only PG
  2. Mechanical Vibrations - S. S. Rao
    1. Do not afraid - Preferable for only PG

List is not exhaustive. Please don’t hesitate to comment your question or suggestions
